Top activities to do in Kolkata

Durga Pujo

Experience the grandeur of Durga Puja, recognized by UNESCO as part of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity. With its artistic pandals and vibrant cultural performances, this festival transcends religion, fostering community spirit and bringing people together through devotion, music, and feasting.

Ride A boat in the holy river of Ganges

Boating on the Ganga in Kolkata offers a serene experience, with views of historic ghats and iconic landmarks, capturing the river’s deep cultural and spiritual significance to the city.

Explore the alley's of North Kolkata

Stroll through the enchanting alleys of North Kolkata, lined with historic buildings that echo the city's rich heritage and timeless charm.

Buy a book from Boi para

Buying books from College Street is more than just a purchase—it’s an experience steeped in nostalgia. As you wander through endless stalls in Asia’s largest book market, the smell of old pages and the charm of handwritten notes evoke a sense of discovery and connection to Kolkata, the intellectual capital of India, and its rich literary soul.
